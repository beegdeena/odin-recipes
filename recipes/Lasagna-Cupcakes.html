Lasagna Cupcakes!

MMmmhmmmmhmmmhm yummy wummy wowo oh mama do we love the goddamn little delicious crusty cupcakes!

It's the perfect dessert for a post-lasagna breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients Required for Lasagna cupcakes

  1. Lasagna leftovers
  2. Brownie flour
  3. Brown sugar
  4. molasses
  5. cupcake pan

Instructions

  1. blend Lasagna leftovers until completely smooth
  2. Add brownie flour, brown sugar, and molasses to the blender
  3. blend again until smooth
  4. add blender contents to cupcake pan until spilling over
  5. bake (DON'T PREHEAT) at 500 degrees C for 600 minutes (ten hours)
  6. eat immediately for best results
