Ready to enjoy a classic cat treat for feline fettuccini fucks? Yummy nummy lasagna in only 3 hours!

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna pasta
  2. Tomatoes' Sauce
  3. Cheez Nuts
  4. Cat food (for flavor)

Instructions

  1. Add the lasagna pasta to heat and water
  2. Burn the lasagna pasta for 20 minutes
  3. Burn the tomato sauce on top of the pasta
  4. Burn the cheez nuts into the tomtato sauce
  5. Burn the cat food for flavor on top of the cheez
  6. Burn the whole lasagna for an additional 2 hours and thirty 5 minutes
